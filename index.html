Karaoke Setlist

Using both the Spotify & Lyrics API, the user logs onto their Spotify account and searches for songs they wish to sing along to. The setlist is also saved to Firebase so they may continue with it at a later time.

Food Blog

A blog front-end designed to replace my wife and I's current blog site. Ability to create new posts with basic formatting and filtering posts based on category. Posts are currently saved to / retrieved from Firebase

Recipe Finder

Using the Yummly API, I have created an app to search for recipes based on name or ingredient. The list can then be further filtered down by course type, cuisine type or dietary restriction. Clicking on the recipe summary will take you to the full recipe listing.

Yahtzee

My take on the classic dice game, Yahtzee, built using Javascript / jQuery and CSS Grids to provide a fun and enjoyable experience to users on all devices.

Responsive PSD Conversion
